1. Executive Project Summary & Business Context

A fatal flaw in aftermarket distribution mapping is the assumption that geographic area equals revenue potential. To move from descriptive mapping to prescriptive revenue capture, I engineered a Geospatial Intelligence dashboard that integrates a Vehicles in Operation (VIO) Penetration Protocol. By taking the effective radius of a branch, analyzing the demographic density, and extracting the estimated volume of 10+ year old prime target vehicles (e.g., 2011-2015 Silverados/F-150s), the algorithm generates live Market Capture percentages to direct Sales and Pricing interventions.
