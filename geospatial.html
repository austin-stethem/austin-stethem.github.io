1. Executive Project Summary & Business Context

In a large-scale distribution network, branch performance is frequently misunderstood due to "territorial blindness"—the assumption that service areas are uniform. This leads to inefficient tiered pricing and missed expansion opportunities. I architected a Geospatial Intelligence engine that overlays competitive density rings onto our existing account base, providing the Sales and Marketing teams with a live, filterable "Heat Map" of margin vulnerability and greenfield potential.

2. Interactive Defense & Expansion Dashboard

A static map is merely a picture; a dynamic map is a business tool. By allowing Regional Directors to filter by specific competitor threats, isolate Top 20% high-volume fleet accounts, and adjust the elasticity radius, this dashboard instantly highlights where margin protection is required and where aggressive acquisition can safely occur.
