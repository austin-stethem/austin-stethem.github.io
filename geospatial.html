1. Executive Project Summary & Business Context

In a large-scale distribution network, protecting existing high-margin business is just as critical as acquiring new accounts. Traditional systems suffer from "territorial blindness," reacting to margin erosion only after volume has dropped. I architected a Geospatial Intelligence engine that overlays competitive density rings onto both our physical hubs and our Top 20% fleet accounts. This provides Sales and Pricing teams with a live, predictive "Heat Map" of vulnerability.
