1. Executive Project Summary & Business Context

In a large-scale distribution network, protecting existing high-margin business is just as critical as acquiring new accounts. Traditional systems suffer from "territorial blindness," reacting to margin erosion only after volume has dropped. I architected a Geospatial Intelligence engine that overlays competitive density rings onto both our physical hubs and our Top 20% fleet accounts. This provides Sales and Pricing teams with a live, predictive "Heat Map" of vulnerability across all market topologies—from dense urban cores to isolated rural routes.

2. Statewide Defense & Expansion Dashboard

By adjusting the elasticity radius below, the engine mathematically cross-references our physical assets and high-value accounts against active competitors in real-time. Each competitor carries a distinct mathematical threat multiplier in the backend algorithm based on their pricing aggression. This dashboard instantly dictates where aggressive acquisition can safely occur, and where margin-lock protocols must be deployed immediately.
