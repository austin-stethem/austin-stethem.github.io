Executive Summary

Managing 50,000 SKUs manually is impossible. Standard practice has been to apply "Blanket Margin" strategies (e.g., all parts get 22%), but this leads to silent revenue loss: we consistently overcharge on high-velocity KVIs (driving away market share) and undercharge on the long-tail (missing margin capture).

This predictive model replaces blanket strategy with Granular Elasticity Modeling. By identifying the specific price sensitivity of every SKU, we "squeeze" the tail for maximum margin while securing the KVI floor to protect competitive standing.
