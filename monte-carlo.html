Algorithm Parameters & Logic

Adjusting the parameters dynamically alters the simulated freight overhead across the database.

The model employs a bottom-up volatility absorption algorithm to ensure P95 risk floors are maintained without triggering price adjustments on top-tier KVIs.

Execution: Margin leakage is injected into Group C inventory first. Once Group C reaches its maximum price-elasticity threshold, the remaining cost burden spills over into Group B, shielding Group A volume-drivers for as long as mathematically viable.
