Executive Narrative: Granular SKU Optimization

Enterprise pricing is traditionally reactive, relying on blanket ABC/KVI categorization. This creates systemic inefficiency: we unknowingly overcharge high-visibility items (risking market share) while "leaving money on the table" across the massive long-tail of Group C parts.

$$Expected Margin = \frac{Sale Price - (Base Cost \cdot (1 + Comp Erosion) + Freight Volatility)}{Sale Price}$$

This engine replaces the manual blanket approach with a live, SQL-fed pricing pipeline. By feeding real-time sales and cost data directly into this Monte Carlo simulation, we move from monthly "gut-check" adjustments to a dynamic model that runs monthly line checkups. This allows us to:

  • Automate Optimization: Micro-adjust prices for 50,000+ SKUs to hit specific target goals without manual intervention.
  • Stress-Test Real-Time: Instantly simulate our response to extreme stress, such as unforecasted tariff hikes, shipping delays, or aggressive competitor encroachment.
  • Proactive Hedging: Identify margin leakage before the month-end P&L reveals the damage.
