Executive Narrative: Granular SKU Optimization

Enterprise pricing is traditionally reactive, relying on blanket ABC/KVI categorization. This creates systemic inefficiency: we unknowingly overcharge high-visibility items (risking market share) while "leaving money on the table" across the massive long-tail of Group C parts.
