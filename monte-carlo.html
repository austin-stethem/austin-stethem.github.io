1. The Strategic Blindspot

Traditional pricing models rely on reactive intuition and flat margin structures. When a vendor issues a sudden price increase or freight costs spike, standard ERP systems often apply a blanket markup across the board. This creates massive risk: it instantly bleeds volume on highly sensitive anchor items (driving customers to competitors) while leaving margin on the table for elastic, low-risk parts.
